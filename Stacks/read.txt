Stacks

:- It is ordered collection of items.
:- It Follows LIFO (last in Fist Out).
:- The addition of new items or the removal of exisisting takes place at the same end.



             BACIC OPERATIONS
     push() -- pushing (storing) an element on the Stacks
     pop() -- Removing (accessing) an element of the Stack 
     peek() -- getting the top element of the stack, without removing it 
     isFull() -- check if the stack is full.
     isEmpty() -- check if the stack is empty
